Site Name

Project Subject: Zumpango Ward Resource Center

Purpose: A page about the Zumpango Ward Resource Center where you can publish activities of Barrio Zumpango and also see the social networks of the neighborhood and even see some tips on the applications of the Church, as well as the catalog of books in the library, and the address of the chapel.

Description: This website will serve as an informative platform for people living in or visiting the Zumpango Ward. It will include upcoming events, resources, and useful information for members and visitors.

Site Purpose

The main purpose of this site is to provide a central hub for residents and visitors to learn about the activities, services, and opportunities available at the Zumpango Ward Resource Center.

  • List current and upcoming activities
  • Show resources like the community library and service opportunities
  • Share tips on how to use Church apps effectively
  • Provide links to local social networks
  • Display book catalog from the library
  • Show chapel location and meeting schedules

Scenarios

These are questions that users might ask when visiting the site:

  • What are the upcoming activities at the Ward?
  • How can I access the Ward Library catalog?
  • Where is the chapel located?
  • Are there any service opportunities available?

Color Schema

  • Accent: #e3f9d1 – used in backgrounds and highlights
  • Neutral: #f8efe7 – used for text containers
  • Secondary: #74b557 – used for buttons and links

Typography

Main Font: Georgia – used for titles and general content

Alternative Font: Times New Roman – used for secondary text and small paragraphs

Font Philosophy: Both fonts are classic, elegant, and readable. They convey tradition and trust — values aligned with the church and community.
